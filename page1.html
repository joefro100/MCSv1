About Us


Metal Cabinet Sales are leading manufacturers of sheet metal enclosures. Established in 1982 we have built a reputation for quality, service and reliability.

Situated in Yeadon, an area known for its rich medhival history, just outside the city of Leeds. Our business is just off the A658, and is next to Leeds Bradford International Airport.

Who We Are

Company Profile


Metal Cabinet Sales are a UK based manufacturers and suppliers of metal cabinets, designed for a wide variety of applications. Situated in Yeadon, an area known for its rich medhival history, just outside the city of Leeds. Our business is just off the A658, and is next to Leeds Bradford International Airport.

Accreditation


Our company has had a number of accreditations, including the ISO9001:2015 Standard, and Link-up Registration Certification for the Supply of General Enclosures. We also offer sheet metal fabrication services, with our welding shop boasting a team of highly trained welders. 

Commitment


Our commitment in these areas is supported by our continued investment in the latest CNC machinery. Together with an experienced and flexible work force this has created an efficient manufacturing unit dedicated to customer satisfaction from inception of order through to final delivery. 

What We Do





MCS offer a wide range of services in sheet metal design and manufacture. We are able to produce items from a bespoke one off design to high volume batch work. These range from simple chassis plates and brackets to fully assembled electrical cabinets and enclosure systems.

How We Do It





The company employs many long serving staff, each with many years’ experience in the design and production of sheet steel enclosures and fabrications. Our dedicated management team will ensure that your specific requirements are met with competitive prices and prompt deliveries.
